FAQ

Questions answered

What data does CancunAI actually collect? +
We collect anonymised browsing signals — pages visited, time spent, and general search behaviour. If you opt in to our feedback program, we also collect your ratings of AI-generated content. We never collect passwords, financial credentials, private messages, or any sensitive personal information. You control what you share.
How do I earn points? +
Points are earned three ways: through passive browsing (the extension runs quietly in the background), through optional AI feedback tasks (rating AI responses), and through referrals (inviting others to join). Points accumulate daily based on your activity level. For example, 10,000 browsing points converts to approximately $14.
How and when do I get paid? +
Withdrawals are processed in crypto — USDT and other supported tokens. Payouts are triggered once your points reach a minimum withdrawal threshold. Earnings are not guaranteed and depend entirely on revenue generated from data buyers. Any figures shown on the site are illustrative estimates, not promises. First payouts are targeted for Q3 2026.
Is my data private and safe? +
Yes. All data is anonymised at the point of collection — before it ever reaches our servers. You explicitly opt in to every data category. You can pause or permanently remove your data at any time by uninstalling the extension. We are GDPR-aware and never share personally identifiable information with any third party.
What is the referral program? +
When you join the waitlist, you get a personal share link. Every person who joins through your link earns you bonus points when we go live. There is no cap — refer as many people as you want. Referral rewards are paid out alongside your regular contribution earnings.
When does CancunAI launch? +
We are currently in the waitlist stage. Private beta opens Q2 2026 to waitlist members first. Public launch is targeted for Q3 2026. Joining the waitlist now guarantees you early access and a launch bonus multiplier on your first earnings — available to the first 1,000 members only.

Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026

What We Collect

When you use CancunAI, we collect anonymised browsing signals — including pages visited, time spent on pages, and general search behaviour. If you opt in to our feedback program, we also collect your ratings of AI-generated content. We do not collect passwords, financial credentials, private messages, or any sensitive personal information.

Waitlist Data

When you join the waitlist, we collect only your email address. This is used solely to notify you when early access opens and to send product updates. We do not share your email with third parties and you can unsubscribe at any time.

How We Use It

Browsing and feedback data is aggregated, anonymised, and supplied to AI companies, research organisations, and ML labs to help train and improve their models. Your data is never sold with personally identifiable information attached. It is processed in bulk alongside thousands of other contributors.

Your Consent

You explicitly opt in to every category of data collection when you install the CancunAI extension. You can pause or permanently stop all collection at any time by uninstalling the extension. Uninstalling immediately halts all data transmission.

Data Storage

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Behavioural data is anonymised at the point of collection before it reaches our servers. We retain anonymised data for up to 24 months. Waitlist and account data is retained until you request deletion.

Third Parties

We work with AI companies, research institutions, and ML labs as data buyers. These parties receive only anonymised, aggregated datasets — never your name, email, or any identifying information. We do not share your data with advertisers under any circumstances.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, reach out via our Telegram community at t.me/cancunAI. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users before they take effect.
