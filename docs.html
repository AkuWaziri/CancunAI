01 What is CancunAI?

CancunAI is a data contribution platform that pays users in crypto for the browsing data they already generate every day.

Users install a browser extension. The extension runs silently in the background, collecting anonymised browsing signals with the user's full consent. Those signals are packaged and sold to AI companies and research labs who need real human behavioural data to train their models. Users earn points for every contribution — points convert to crypto (USDT and others) once a minimum threshold is reached.

The platform is currently in waitlist stage. The first 1,000 members get early access, a launch bonus multiplier, and the ability to earn points before public launch through Twitter posts and the upcoming AI feedback task campaign.

One sentence version: CancunAI pays you in crypto for data your browser already generates — passively, privately, every day.

02 How Points Work

Points are the internal currency of CancunAI. They accumulate based on your activity and convert to crypto once you hit the minimum withdrawal threshold. Here's how you earn them:

🌐
Browsing — passive, automatic Every page you visit while the extension is active earns points. Example: 10,000 browsing points ≈ $14 in crypto value. Starts at extension launch.
𝕏
Twitter posts — active now Post about CancunAI on Twitter at least twice a week. Submit your tweet URL on the site. Each verified post earns 500 points. Points visible soon after verification.
👥
Referrals — unlimited Every person who joins via your personal referral link earns you bonus points at launch. No cap. The more you refer, the more you earn.
⚡
AI Feedback Tasks — early testers only Rate AI responses, label content, compare outputs. Available to waitlist members before public launch. Earns 30–300 points per task depending on complexity.

Important: Point values and conversion rates are not guaranteed. Actual payouts depend entirely on revenue generated from AI data buyers. Any figures shown are illustrative estimates, not promises.

05 Tone of Voice

CancunAI speaks like a sharp, honest founder — not a hype machine. Keep posts direct, confident, and grounded.

Direct

Say exactly what it does. No fluff. No buzzwords. Short sentences work best.

Confident

Don't hedge. "You can earn" is weak. "You earn" is strong. Own the message.

Honest

Never promise guaranteed earnings. The opportunity is real — the amount varies. Say that.

Human

Talk to real people. Avoid corporate language. Write how you'd explain it to a friend.
